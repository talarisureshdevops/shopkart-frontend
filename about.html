Our Story

From a Small Garage to a Global Marketplace

It started in 2018 with a simple dream: create a shopping platform that truly puts the customer first.

ShopElite was born when our founder, Michael Chen, struggled to find a trustworthy online store that offered both premium quality and transparent pricing. Frustrated by his experiences, he decided to build the solution himself — in his garage with a team of 3.

Today, ShopElite employs over 200 people across 5 offices worldwide and serves customers in 50+ countries. But our core values haven't changed: quality, transparency, and putting our customers at the heart of everything we do.

2018 Founded in New York
2020 Reached 10,000 customers
2022 International expansion
2024 50,000+ customers worldwide
