Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. You'll find real-time tracking information and carrier details there. You'll also receive tracking updates via email.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and changes may not be possible. Contact our support team immediately if you need to make changes.

We offer Standard (3-5 days, $5.99), Express (1-2 days, $14.99), and Same-Day delivery (select areas, $24.99). Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping.

Yes! We ship to 50+ countries. International shipping rates and times vary by destination. Duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

We offer free 30-day returns on most items. Items must be in original condition with all tags and packaging. Start a return from your account dashboard or contact our support team.

Once we receive your return, refunds are processed within 2-3 business days. The time for funds to appear in your account depends on your payment method (typically 3-7 days for credit cards).

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and ShopElite Gift Cards. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.
